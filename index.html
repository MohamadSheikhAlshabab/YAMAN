ABOUT

Al- Yaman Metal Machining is a small innovative CNC-machining company based in Jordan, with its origins and assets moved from Syria in 2013. We have a rich history of accomplishments producing complex mechanical systems and spare parts made for different purposes and working with both regional and local partners since 2004. From nutrition and medical to oil and mobile industries, we are proud of providing a wide range of high-quality integrated products to support such industries over the last decade. Our professional and customized designs were used to tackle complicated problems in the production lines of many factories addressing their urgent needs. The superior precision and accuracy of our products are highly valued by our clients and are continuously defining us as a leading competitor in the local market. With a long-term strategic plan of improving the local CNC-machining industry, we are aiming to extend our business to a wider range of advanced machinery reflecting our deep faith in a brighter CNC-machining future. We are pleased to invite interested clients to call us for a consultation or to visit us at our location.
